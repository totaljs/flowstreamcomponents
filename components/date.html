The component will return a Date/Time object (raw or formatted) according to its configuration.
Date format
Empty value will return current datetime object.

d - day e.g. 1
dd - day e.g. 01
ddd - day (named). MO
dddd - day (named). Monday
M - month e.g. 1
MM - month e.g. 01
MMM - month (named). Jan
MMMM - month (named). January
yy - year e.g. 16
yyyy - year e.g. 2016
w - week number e.g. 1
ww - week number e.g. 01
H - hour e.g. 1
HH - hour e.g. 01
m - minute e.g. 1
mm - minute e.g. 01
s - second e.g. 1
ss - second e.g. 01
a - AM/PM
